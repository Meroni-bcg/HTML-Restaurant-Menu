Origin Story

Las Vegas 2008, all we had was $20 for a week of dinners. My family created low cost flavorful meals and shared some with locals whom were experiencing hunger in those hard times. After the economy came back up we decided to make a family dining establishment with the meals that got us through a harsh recession.
